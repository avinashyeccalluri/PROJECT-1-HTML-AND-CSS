Acute Kidney Injury

What is Acute Kidney Injury?

It is the sudden damage to the kidneys that will exist only for a short time. But there are chances for the injury to remain for a longer period too. It may be different ways

  • There is a chance that the injury might be totally healed and the person will have the proper functioning of Kidney
  • Partial healing of the injury affecting only certain functions of kidney with no necessary for a dialysis
  • Complete damage to the kidneys and in need of a dialysis

What are the symptoms?

The identification of the injury involves

  • Reduced levels of urine
  • Increasing level of creatinine
  • Rapid build up of fluid level in the body
  • Increase in the toxin levels within the body

What are the causes?

  • A sudden drop in flow of blood in urine
  • Damages can also be happening because of the usage of medicines
  • There may be a sudden blockage of urine from the kidneys
  • Certain long time health problems can also be a cause of it
  • Surgeries in heart or bone marrow at ICU can make a person to be a victim to the problems

Preventive measures

A good solution to have a good health if kidney is to have a regular check up to know about the problems that we have.
