Treatment Of Kidneys

A good control of blood sugar level and blood pressure can prevent the person being a victim to glomerulonephritis.

The treatment if the kidney diseases may vary in accordance with the severity. That is it depends on the stage in which the disease has risen. We can divide the stage into 3

  • Early stage
  • Middle stage
  • Later stage

Earlier Stages:

In the earlier stage of the disease, the severity will be very less. So the treatment will be focused on slowing the severity of kidney disease. There will be treatments that would reduce the occurrence of the cardiovascular diseases. If you come to know that you may be affected by any of the kidney disease then you will have to change the lifestyle that will help to reduce the development of the disease. The medications prescribed by the doctor will also be less when half of the work is done by the changes on the lifestyle. A regular kidney health check will also be done by the doctor on regular basis to keep on track about the disease.

Middle Stages:

The middle stage has the risk of developing bone diseases and anemia. Because the stagnant of waste caused by the chronic kidney disease will start to increase. The focus of treatment will be as same as the treatment of the early stage. An extra effort will be taken by the doctors to reduce the complications of the disease. At this stage it is good to approach a kidney specialist who would guide you in the correct way so that the severity of the disease does not improve. Frequent tests of blood and urine will help to track the level of disease.

Later Stages:

This stage can also be called as the end stage of kidney's life or also called as kidney failure (renal failure). This is the case where Kidney transplantation and dialysis plays a major role in the treatment of the affected kidney. Because in this stage the entire function of the kidney may come to halt having a need for the replacement or supplement.
